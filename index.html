All About Cookies

Hello and welcome, tech enthusiast!

On this webpage, we will be discussing everything you'd want to know about cookies! You will learn what internet cookies are, why they were made, and how they are helpful today. Additionally, you will learn about how cookies are used to improve your browsing experience, and even about the importance of security when navigating the web. Internet cookies play a role in helping both users and web developers, so whether you like to browse websites or make them, there are many benefits to learning how cookies operate!

Before you dive in, here are a few keywords you may want to take a look at.

Keyword Definition
Lou Montulli A software engineer who invented HTTP cookies.
HTTP Cookies Used to track and improve a user’s browsing experience.
Personalization Improving a user’s internet experience by tailoring content and settings to their preferences.
Targeted Advertising Displaying unique ads that are relevant to a user’s browsing history.
Persistent Cookies Stored on the user’s hard drive for a certain amount of time. Commonly used for authentication, so that users do not have to re-enter their login credentials.
First Party Cookies Commonly used by advertisers and analytics companies to track a user’s browsing history.
Virtual Private Network (VPN) Software that helps to encrypt your data and creates a secure, private connection between your device and a network.
